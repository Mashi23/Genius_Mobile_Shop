----------------- Genius Mobile Web-Based Shop Management System -------------------

## Overview

The Genius Mobile web-based shop management system is designed to streamline and enhance the management of a mobile phone shop.
The system includes functionalities for online ordering, customer and employee management, product and inventory management, transaction tracking, and supplier and account management. 
The user panel functions as a Point of Sale (POS) system for in-store transactions.

## Features

### Admin Panel
- **Customer Management**: Add, edit, delete, and view customer details.
- **Employee Management**: Add, edit, delete, and view employee details.
- **Product Management**: Add, edit, delete, and view product details.
- **Inventory Management**: View and update inventory details.
- **Transaction Management**: View all transactions.
- **Supplier Management**: Add, edit, delete, and view supplier details.
- **Account Management**: Add new users and manage user details.

### User Panel (POS)
- **Product Category Browsing**: Browse through various product categories.
- **Add to Cart**: Add products to the cart and calculate subtotal, taxes, and total amount due.
- **Customer Management**: Search for existing customers or create new customer profiles.
- **Payment Processing**: Enter payment amount, process payments, and generate receipts.
- **Receipt Handling**: Print receipts for customers; transaction history is accessible only by admins.

### Customer Online Ordering
- **Home Page**: Browse and search for mobile phones.
- **Cart Management**: Add products to cart and calculate total payment.
- **Payment Gateway**: Process payments and place orders.
- **Order Management**: Options to continue or cancel orders.
- **About Us**: Contact form for inquiries and feedback.
